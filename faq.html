General Questions

What is Marriage.lk?

Marriage.lk is the fastest growing No.01 Digital wedding portal in Sri Lanka. Our platform connects local weddings & event professionals to prospective couples who admires new experiences by planning their wedding via online.

How does l find out about up-coming events?

You sign-up to our newsletter and once a month we email a showcase special with the up-coming showcases.

Vendor

How do I register as a wedding vendor?

Click Vendor and fill in details as requested and sign up. You will be redirected to the Dashboard.

How can I Show case my services?

You can showcase services in vendor’s wedding page in addition registered users can sign-up to our newsletter and once a month we email a suppliers’ up-coming showcases.

Payment Procedure?

End of each month Marriage.lk will issue an invoice and supplier will need to do online transaction.

How do I Subscribe a package?

At the registration select a subscription plan, as per request supplier can upgrade or degrade the package upon the admin approval.

Bride & Groom

How do I register as a user?

Click User and fill in details as requested and sign up. You will be redirected to the Homepage.

How do l contact and book a wedding venue/vendor?

You simply enquire from wedding suppliers using the online chat box via their listing

Once l has booked a venue/vendor who will correspond?

You speak directly to the wedding professional or venue about your needs. We simply connect you to experienced, wedding professionals verified in our wedding portal.

How do l arrange an inspection before payment?

You can simply request for quotation and schedule an appointment via Messenger providing the required details.
